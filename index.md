# Welcome to Book Hub

**Your personal space for discovering, reviewing, and sharing great books.**

Book Hub is a curated reading companion built for book lovers of all kinds. Whether you enjoy fiction, non-fiction, science, history, or fantasy, this site brings together honest reviews, genre guides, and handpicked recommendations to help you find your next great read.

---

## What You'll Find Here

- **Favorite Books** — A curated list of must-read titles across multiple genres.
- **Reviews** — Honest, student-written reviews of popular and classic books.
- **Genres** — Browse books organized by genre with reading suggestions.
- **About** — Learn more about the person behind Book Hub.
- **Contact** — Get in touch with questions, suggestions, or book recommendations.

## Why Book Hub?

I built Book Hub as a companion to my full-stack book management application. That app lets users browse, rate, and track their reading progress in real time. This static site distills the same love of reading into a simple, clean format — perfect for sharing recommendations and reviews without needing an account.
